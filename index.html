Our Business

Nanji Chicken Rice Shop aims to provide customers a delicious chicken rice. The restaurant serves guests throughout the region. Over the years, we have continued to maintain the taste, in order to provide customers with a selection of delicious dishes, and to ensure that they can enjoy an unforgettable dining experience at Nanji Chicken Rice Shop.

Our Menu

The Chicken Restaurant offers traditionally made streamed, grilled, and black sauce chicken rice as its signature product. These signatures dishes are paired with fragrant chicken-flavored rice and winter melon soup. The most famous chicken rice shop is black oil sauce chicken rice. This black oil sauce chicken rice is refined from cooking recipes handed down from the previous generation.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide customers with a memorable experiences by providing the best food, and service in Nanji Rice Shop.

Our vision is to expland our business in other states of Malaysia.
